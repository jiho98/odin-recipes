Shepherd’s Pie

Description

Shepherd’s Pie is a traditional Irish dish that consists of ground beef (or lamb) with vegetables in a rich gravy, topped with cheesy mashed potatoes, and baked.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (or lamb)
  • Dried parsley
  • Dried thyme
  • Dried rosemary
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Beef broth
  • Frozen vegetables
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic powder
  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Tomato paste
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Sauté some chopped onions in olive oil.
  2. Add ground beef or ground lamb.
  3. Add dried parsley, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir and cook the meat mixture until the meat is browned.
  5. Add minced garlic and Worcestershire sauce.
  6. Cook the garlic for a minute.
  7. Add flour and tomato paste.
  8. Add beef broth, frozen mixed peas and carrots, and frozen corn kernels.
  9. Simmer the meat gravy until it is thick.
  10. Boil some peeled and chopped potatoes until they are soft.
  11. Drain the potatoes.
  12. Add butter, half & half, parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  13. Mash the potatoes until smooth.
  14. Pour the meat mixture into a casserole dish.
  15. Top it with the cheesy mashed potatoes.
  16. Bake until the meat is bubbling and the potatoes are golden.
