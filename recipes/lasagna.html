Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is made with layers of meat, onion, tomato, sugar, seasonings, lasagna noodles, cheeses, and sometimes eggs. It's typically baked in the oven and served hot. This lasagna recipe is made with ground beef, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese. It's a hearty and satisfying dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
