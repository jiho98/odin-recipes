Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

This recipe shows you how to make homemade meatballs with a delicious and fresh tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Bread crumbs
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt & Sugar
  • Bay leaf
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make the meatballs.
  2. Make the sauce.
  3. Add the meatballs.
